It is November 1932 in Australia. The Australian military has just declared war on the emus. 

The Australian Office of National Intelligence has gathered information on locations where emus are gathering to eat farmers crops.

You must place soldiers to guard these areas and defeat the attacking emus.


For assistance in the war effort, the Australian government has summoned military personnel and equipment from the future,
including Apache helicopters, which shoot AGM-114 hellfire missiles


No Emus were harmed in the making of this game.


Parts of this story are fabricated, the true story can be read here:
